Education

Galileo's World celebrates the University of Oklahoma's 125th birthday with a series of exhibitions and programs across campus beginning August 2015.

 

The University of Oklahoma’s "Galileo’s World" exhibition brings together the vast range of natural sciences and the broad spectrum of the humanities. Relating the history of science to the present and future and exploring the cultural significance of scientific discovery, “Galileo's World” will have exhibits at the Bizzell Memorial Library, the Fred Jones Jr. Museum of Art, the Sam Noble Oklahoma Museum of Natural History, and the National Weather Center.
